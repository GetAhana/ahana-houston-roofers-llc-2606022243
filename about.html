About us

Our story
Why we do this.

We started Houston Roofers to give homeowners one trusted crew for repairs, storms, and full replacements. Our family has been in the roofing trade since the 90s, and we bring that foundation and integrity to every job.

From leak tracing and emergency tarping to major tear-offs and commercial coating systems, we handle the full scope. We listen, explain options clearly, and never pressure you into work you don't need.

Whether it's photo-documented insurance claims or routine maintenance, we show up on time, work clean, and treat your home like our own. That's how we've earned 4.8 stars and a reputation across Houston.
