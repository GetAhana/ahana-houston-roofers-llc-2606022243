Roof replacement

Full roof replacement in Houston—tear-offs, new systems, and attic ventilation checks

Storm damage, age, or wear calls for a complete tear-off and re-roof. We inspect attic ventilation, use manufacturer-backed shingles or metal systems, and magnet-sweep the yard before you pay.

Roof replacement systems and standards

  • Asphalt shingles: 30–50-year manufacturer ratings with attic intake vents
  • Metal roofing: 140+ mph wind rating, standing seam or exposed fastener
  • Ice-and-water shield in valleys and eaves per code
  • Magnet sweep of yard; gutters cleaned before final walkthrough

Over 18 years serving Houston families; 52 verified reviews averaging 4.8 stars on roof replacements across The Woodlands, Pearland, and Sugar Land.

How we handle Roof replacement

Our roof replacement process takes most homes 2–5 days from tear-off to final cleanup.

  • Inspection and written estimate — We visit, assess the existing roof and attic ventilation, take photos, and send a detailed estimate before any work. No pressure; we explain full costs upfront.
  • Tear-off and installation — Crew removes old roofing, inspects the deck, installs new sheathing or repairs as needed, then lays underlayment and shingles or metal per manufacturer specs.
  • Cleanup, inspection, and final walkthrough — Magnet sweep removes loose nails and debris. We inspect gutters, explain the warranty, and you approve the work before final payment.
When to call us

Watch for these signs your roof may need replacement instead of repair.

  • Multiple missing or curled shingles across several areas of the roof
  • Visible daylight or water stains in the attic during rain
  • Sagging or soft spots when walked, especially after a storm
  • Granule loss evident in gutters or a thin, worn appearance
  • Previous roof over 20 years old with no underlay upgrade
