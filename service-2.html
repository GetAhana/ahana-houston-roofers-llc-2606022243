Roof repair

Roof leak and shingle repair in Houston—fast response, insurance documentation

Leaks, flashing failures, and storm damage don't wait. We trace the source, replace shingles or flashing, and can tarp emergency damage same day. Photos ready for your adjuster.

Repair scope and emergency standards

  • Leak tracing: water intrusion location pinpointed with attic inspection and photos
  • Shingle and flashing repair: matching materials when color match available; new shingles on visible repairs
  • Emergency tarping: 24-hour availability for storm damage; tarp rated for 60+ mph wind
  • Insurance documentation: before and after photos, damage assessment notes provided

Responds to leak and storm calls within 24 hours most days; 18+ years fixing Houston roofs in all seasons from spring rain to summer downpours.

How we handle Roof repair

Emergency and routine repair calls follow a clear sequence—most jobs complete in one visit.

  • Same-day or next-morning call response — Text or call with photos if you have them. We schedule an inspection window and send realistic arrival times; for active leaks indoors, we discuss temporary tarping options.
  • On-site inspection and estimate — Crew inspects roof, attic, and water path; documents damage with photos; explains repair vs. replacement options; and sends written estimate before work begins.
  • Repair and cleanup — Crew repairs shingles, flashing, or other damage; cleans debris from yard; takes final photos for insurance; and explains what your policy may cover.
When to call us

These signs indicate a roof repair may be needed soon.

  • Water stains on ceilings or walls after rain, especially in corners or valleys
  • Shingles lifted or curled at edges, or flashing pulling away from walls
  • Visible cracks, tears, or missing pieces after storms or high wind events
  • Gutters overflowing despite being clear, or water pooling near foundation
  • Musty smell in attic or insulation damp to touch during dry weather
