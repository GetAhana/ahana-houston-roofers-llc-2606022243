Storm damage

Hail and wind damage inspection in Houston—insurance claim support

Hail and high-wind events damage roofs fast. We photograph damage, document conditions for your adjuster, and explain repair-or-replace options without sales pressure.

Storm damage assessment and documentation

  • Hail damage mapping: size and pattern of impacts documented with GPS location and date
  • Wind damage evaluation: missing shingles, exposed nails, flashing separation, and structural concerns noted
  • Insurance-ready report: photos tagged by location, damage descriptions, and repair estimates provided
  • Adjuster coordination: we can meet the insurance inspector and discuss findings

Over 200 storm damage assessments in Houston metro; 4.8-star average from families who recovered claims quickly with our documentation.

How we handle Storm damage

Storm damage assessment is fast and thorough—most inspections complete in one visit.

  • Rapid response and initial photos — We schedule visits within 48 hours of your call. Crew photographs the roof, attic, and visible damage; marks problem areas; and notes weather conditions and time of loss.
  • Detailed damage report and insurance coordination — Written report with photos, damage measurements, and repair scope is sent to you and your insurer. We offer to meet the adjuster and discuss findings in person if needed.
  • Repair guidance and estimates — Once your claim is approved, we provide repair or replacement estimates, explain warranty options, and schedule work with realistic timelines.
When to call us

After a storm, look for these signs of roof damage.

  • Visible dents or cracks on shingles, especially in a pattern matching hail size
  • Missing shingles or bare spots exposing the underlying layer
  • Flashing bent, separated, or missing fasteners around vents or walls
  • Gutters dented, and downspouts damaged or knocked loose
  • Interior ceiling leaks or water stains appearing after heavy wind or hail
