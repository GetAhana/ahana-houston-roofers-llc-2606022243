Commercial roofing

Commercial roof repair and maintenance—retail and light industrial

Retail shops, offices, and light industrial properties need reliable low-slope roofing. We repair leaks, apply coatings, and manage maintenance plans to extend roof life and avoid surprise failures.

Commercial roofing systems and maintenance scope

  • Low-slope membranes: TPO, EPDM, and modified bitumen inspection and repair
  • Coating systems: acrylic and polyurethane coatings for added durability and reflectivity
  • Maintenance plans: scheduled inspections, sealant touch-ups, and gutter cleaning on contract
  • Penetration sealing: vents, HVAC boots, and pipe flashing sealed to prevent water intrusion

Serves 50+ commercial properties in Houston, Pearland, and Sugar Land; preventive maintenance clients report 30–40% fewer emergency calls than reactive-only competitors.

How we handle Commercial roofing

Commercial roof work is scheduled to minimize disruption—most inspections and repairs happen during off-hours or low-traffic windows.

  • On-site evaluation and cost estimate — We inspect your entire roof, identify problem areas, assess membrane condition, and provide a written repair or maintenance proposal with no obligation.
  • Planned maintenance or repair execution — Work is scheduled during your preferred window. Crew completes repairs, applies coatings if approved, cleans debris, and provides a completion report.
  • Ongoing maintenance scheduling — We recommend annual inspections and touch-ups to extend roof life. Quarterly or semi-annual plans are available; we send reminders and coordinate with your facility team.
When to call us

Commercial roofs show wear differently—watch for these signals.

  • Pooling water on roof surface that remains hours after rain
  • Blistering or bubbling in membrane, or visible seams pulling apart
  • Water leaks appearing in multiple locations on the same roof section
  • Interior ceiling leaks or stains in back rooms or storage areas
  • Roof age over 15 years with no prior maintenance or coating applied
