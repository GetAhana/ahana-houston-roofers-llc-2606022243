Roof inspections

Pre-sale, post-storm, and annual roof inspections in Houston

Before listing, after a storm, or as preventive maintenance, a professional roof inspection catches problems early. We provide written photo reports so you know exactly what's there—and what's not.

Inspection scope and documentation standards

  • Exterior evaluation: shingle or metal condition, flashing integrity, ridge vents and penetrations assessed
  • Attic inspection: ventilation, insulation, water stains, and structural damage identified
  • Gutters and drainage: assessed for flow, damage, and proper slope
  • Written photo report: dated images, condition notes, and recommended actions included

Completed 300+ inspections for Houston-area homebuyers and homeowners; reports accepted by lenders and title companies across the region.

How we handle Roof inspections

A professional roof inspection takes 1–2 hours and delivers a detailed report within 24 hours.

  • Scheduling and on-site walkthrough — We arrange a time convenient for you. Inspector photographs the roof exterior, climbs the attic safely, documents ventilation and structure, and checks gutters.
  • Photo documentation and condition notes — Inspector captures close-ups of shingles, flashing, vents, and problem areas with date stamps. Attic photos show ventilation, insulation, and any water stains.
  • Written report delivery — You receive a PDF report with photos organized by roof section, condition ratings for each area, and clear recommendations for maintenance or repair.
When to call us

Consider a professional inspection in these situations.

  • You are preparing to sell your home and need a pre-listing roof assessment
  • A recent storm (hail, high wind, or heavy rain) raised concerns about damage
  • You inherited or purchased a home and want to understand the roof condition and age
  • Insurance company requested a formal roof inspection before renewing your policy
  • Your roof is over 15 years old and you want to plan for replacement timing
